Cemetery Safety Protocols

OPERATIONAL SAFETY & CONDUCT | MRKOLAPIE PTY LTD ™

1. Respectful Conduct

Cemeteries are sacred spaces. Visitors are requested to maintain a quiet and respectful demeanor at all times. Please ensure all children are supervised and avoid stepping directly on grave mounds or markers.

2. Physical Safety

Cemetery terrain can be uneven. We advise wearing sturdy footwear. Be aware of your surroundings, especially in older sections of the cemetery where ground stability may vary. Avoid visiting during extreme weather conditions or after sunset.

3. Digital Usage Safety

When using the Qabr Finder AI Scan feature, please remain stationary and aware of your environment. Do not obstruct pathways or the work of cemetery maintenance staff while interacting with the application.

4. Environmental Stewardship

Help us maintain the dignity of these sites. Please dispose of all litter in designated bins and do not remove or disturb any flora, stones, or existing cemetery infrastructure.
