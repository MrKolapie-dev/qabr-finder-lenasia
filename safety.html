Cemetery Safety Protocols

Operational Safety & Conduct | MrKolapie Pty Ltd ™

1. Respectful Conduct

Cemeteries are sacred spaces. Visitors are requested to maintain a quiet and respectful demeanor. Supervise children at all times to ensure their safety and the sanctity of the grounds. No stepping on or walking across grave mounds or markers.

2. Physical Safety

Terrain can be naturally uneven. We advise wearing sturdy footwear and exercising caution. Avoid visiting during extreme weather conditions or after sunset for personal safety.

3. Digital Usage Safety

When using the Qabr Finder AI Scan feature, please remain stationary and aware of your environment. Do not obstruct pathways or the work of maintenance staff while interacting with the application.

4. Environmental Stewardship

Dispose of litter in designated bins. Preservation of the ecosystem is mandatory; do not remove flora or stones from the premises.
